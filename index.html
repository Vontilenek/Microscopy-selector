Step 9
Has your sample been optically cleared?
Clearing removes lipids and homogenises refractive index, dramatically improving light penetration. Only applicable to fixed samples — live samples cannot be cleared.
💧

Step 10
What best describes your sample or imaging goal?
This helps us choose between Cresty and the CSU-W1 — two spinning disk systems with different strengths.
CSU-W1 — microlens array + wider pinhole spacing: less crosstalk, better light collection, superior depth penetration and Z-discrimination for fine structures and thick tissue (>65 µm)
Cresty — water immersion objectives (40x & 60x), broader laser lines (CFP/YFP/Cy3/Cy7), large 25mm FOV, fast acquisition — well suited to tissue up to ~65 µm

🧱

💧
Need water immersion objectives or broad laser lines40x/60x water immersion for live cells, or CFP/YFP/Cy3/Cy7 dyes — Cresty only
